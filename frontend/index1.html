About Malware Detection

Malware detection is the process of identifying and removing malicious software from a computer or network. It involves various techniques such as signature-based detection, behavior analysis, and heuristics.

While HTML and CSS cannot directly detect malware or .exe files, they can be used to create informative websites to educate users about malware risks and prevention measures.

Tips for Safe Browsing

  • Keep your operating system and software up to date.
  • Use a reputable antivirus program and keep it updated.
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading files from unknown sources.
  • Be cautious when opening email attachments, especially from unfamiliar senders.
  • Regularly back up your important files to prevent data loss in case of malware infection.
